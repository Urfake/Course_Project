Personnel Accounting

Personnel Accounting is related to the construction of labor relations between
an employer and an employee. It includes registration of vacations, tax deductions,
hiring and dismissal. Personnel accounting is mandatory for any company, without
exceptions in terms of organizational and legal form and size of staff.

About Us

HR accounting is the work of registering, accounting and monitoring the movement of employees of the company, which is regulated by law. It involves documenting the admission, transfer, and dismissal of employees, sick leave, business trips, timesheets, vacations, personal cards. Some forms are unified, others are developed in the company.

Personnel accounting is kept on the basis of primary personnel documentation and internal regulations of the company. In their work, personnel officers are guided primarily by the Labor Code. Besides him, there are several other basic sources.

Director

Director must see and have access to all reports, the ability to delete, update some expenses, delete, add an employee, raise or lower an employee's salary.

HR Manager

HR Manager should have an idea about employees, add a new employee, delete, make personnel changes, etc.

Worker

Workers of the organization will have to be able to see their work schedule, their wages, if there is a bonus, then see their bonus as well.
